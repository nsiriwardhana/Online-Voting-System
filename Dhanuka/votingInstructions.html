01. Registration:

a. To participate in the voting, you need to register on the website. Click on the "Register" button.
b. Provide the required information, including your name, email address, and any other relevant details.
c. Choose a unique username and password for future login.

02. Login:

a. Once registered, click on the "Login" button on the website's homepage.
b. Enter your username and password to access your account.

03. Placing a Vote:

a. Click on the contestant you wish to vote for. You may only vote for one contestant per voting round.
b. Confirm your selection and submit your vote by clicking on the "Vote" button.
c. Note any restrictions or rules regarding the number of votes you can cast and any time limitations for voting.

04. Leaderboard:

a. The leaderboard section will display the current vote counts and rankings of the contestants.
b. You can check the leaderboard to see the real-time updates on the leading contestant.
